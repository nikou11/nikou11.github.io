I have experience across **consulting, design, and research**, and I enjoy working on problems that combine human insight with technical and strategic thinking.

Through my consulting work, I’ve contributed to digital transformation initiatives, analyzing operational systems and helping design improved digital experiences for clients.  

In design, I’ve developed user-centered digital products, conducting user research and building prototypes that translate insights into practical solutions.

## Selected Work

### Orion Digital Transformation
Consulting strategy case focused on modernizing a wealth management platform.  
The project analyzes current operational challenges and proposes a modern digital ecosystem including client portals, mobile capabilities, and improved advisor workflows.

### Noki – Language Learning App
A UX design project exploring scenario-based language learning through real-world interactions.  
The app focuses on helping users build conversational confidence through story-led tasks, interactive practice, and community engagement.

### Honours Thesis – Lightness Constancy in Virtual Reality
Experimental research investigating how people estimate surface reflectance when illumination conditions change.  
The study uses controlled psychophysical tasks in virtual environments to examine how humans interpret visual information.
